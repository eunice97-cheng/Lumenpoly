Field Manual
Objective
Conquer territories, build fortifications, and drain rival Marshals of their gold. The last Marshal standing — or the wealthiest when the 30-minute timer expires — wins the campaign.
Turn Flow
Each turn begins with a 10-second countdown. If you do not roll in time, Invoke Lumen fires automatically. Rolling doubles lets you roll again — three consecutive doubles send you to the Ban List.
Spaces
Ascension Achieve (GO) — Receive $200 gold when you pass.
Gaeto's Decree — Draw a random decree card.
Bilins' Decree — Draw a vault card.
Territory Tithe / Guild Levy — Pay a tax to the War Council.
Meditation Spot — Safe passage; no effect.
Exploits Detected — Instant warp to the Ban List.
Ban List — Imprisoned up to 3 turns; escape via doubles or a $50 bribe.
Territories & Tribute
Land on an unowned territory to conquer it. If a rival owns it, pay War Reparations. Control every territory in a color domain to double base tribute and unlock fortifications. Transport waypoints scale: 1 hub = $25 → 4 hubs = $200.
Guild Towers & Fortress
Requires full domain control. Cost = 50% of conquest price per upgrade.
4 Guild Towers (N / E / S / W) — tribute × (1 + towers).
Fortress (after all 4 towers) — tribute × 8. Maximum defense.
Even-build rule: all territories in a domain must stay within 1 tower of each other.
Diplomacy
You may Declare Non Hostile instead of paying tribute when landing on a rival's territory. The defending Marshal decides to honor the truce or demand war reparations (auto-demand in 5 s). Disabled in Insane Mode+.
Ban List Rule
If a territory's owner is on the Ban List, they cannot collect tribute. Invaders pass through at zero cost for every turn the owner remains imprisoned.
Trade
Before rolling, propose a territory trade with any active Marshal — offer gold, territories, or both. The rival has 10 seconds to accept (auto-reject on timeout). Disabled in Insane Mode+.
Debt & Bankruptcy
Falling into debt opens the Asset Liquidation panel. Sell towers (25% of build cost) or cede territories (50% of conquest cost). If debt can't be cleared, surrender — all territories revert to neutral.
Escalating Modes
HARD 5 min — taxes & bribe doubled.
ELITE 10 min — only punishing decrees drawn.
INSANE 15 min — no GO bonus, no trade, no diplomacy.
NIGHTMARE 20 min — decree penalties doubled.
HELL 25 min — war reparations doubled.
At 30 minutes: highest net worth wins.
